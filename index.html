WHO WE ARE

A Movement, Not an Institution

CrossCulture Community Foundation (CCF) was established in Texas in 2019 as a 501(c)(3) nonprofit, and in March 2026 was registered as a nonprofit voluntary organization in Seoul, Korea.

Our founder, Rev. Dr. Bill R. Majors, has served as a missionary in Seoul since June 1, 1982. CrossCulture Community is his vision — a center where the Bible is honored as the universal heritage of all humanity.

We are open to all, regardless of faith. Our community is built on horizontal unity — mutual listening, shared stories, and lives transformed by the living Word. The movement is to let the Bible speak for itself.

WHAT WE DO

How We Work

CrossCulture Community is built around three commitments that reflect the original crossing of cultures in the Incarnation.

The Bible as Human Heritage

We present the Bible not as the property of any denomination or tradition, but as a gift to all humanity — the most translated, most distributed book in history.

Stories from the Living Word

We collect and share testimonies — stories FROM the living Word of God — that reveal how scripture transforms lives across every culture and generation.

Open Community, Discipled Board

Membership is free and open to all. Leadership is entrusted to disciples of Jesus who accept the Bible as God's Word — ensuring the mission remains anchored.

THE DREAM AHEAD

Museum of the Bible Korea

대한민국 성경박물관
(MOTB-Korea)

The Museum of the Bible Korea is our capstone vision — a world class place where the Bible's journey as the universal heritage of humanity is celebrated, displayed, and made accessible to all in the heart of downtown Seoul.

We distinguish ourselves from existing Korean church history museums: our focus is the Bible as global human heritage — not representing any religion, denomination or church.

ORGANIZATIONAL DEVELOPMENT

A Four-Step Journey

CrossCulture Community is building its legal foundation in Korea through four stages of nonprofit registration — each step increasing credibility, tax benefits, and institutional capacity.

✅ Complete
01
비영리임의단체
Unincorporated Association

CrossCulture Community established as an organization in Korea. The beginning of legal existence.

🔄 In Progress
02
비영리민간단체
NPNGO Registration

Pursuing registration with the Seoul Metropolitan Government. Grants public interest status and tax benefits for Korean individual donors.

🎯 Next
03
사단법인
Incorporated Association

Pursuing incorporation under the Ministry of Culture, Sports and Tourism. Enables corporate donor tax benefits and access to government grants.

🏛️ Vision
04
재단법인
Foundation

The permanent legal foundation for the Museum of the Bible Korea — holding its collection, building, and endowment in perpetuity.

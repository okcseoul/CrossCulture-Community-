CrossCulture Community is building its legal foundation in Korea through three stages — each step increasing capacity, credibility, and institutional permanence.

02
사단법인
Incorporated Association

Pursuing incorporation under the Ministry of Culture, Sports and Tourism. Permits faith-centered directors while remaining open to the public in a nonsectarian way. Enables corporate donor tax benefits and access to government grants.

LEGAL NOTICE

Protected Provisions

The following provisions of the CrossCulture Community bylaws carry special protection and cannot be altered by ordinary amendment procedures:

Art. 3 (Purpose/Mission) — Cannot be altered without Founding Board unanimity. Art. 25 (Mission Lock) — Supreme protection clause. Art. 26 (Founding Board Protection) — The Founding Board cannot be removed by the General Assembly. Founding Board membership: President is Bill Majors; successor Presidents are appointed by the Founding Board only.

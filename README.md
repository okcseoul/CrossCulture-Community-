The Core Eligibility Requirements
To register, the organization must meet all of these conditions:
The direct beneficiaries of the organization's activities must be the general public (불특정 다수), not specific individuals.
Members must not distribute profits among themselves.
The organization must not exist primarily to support or oppose a political party or electoral candidate.
The organization must not exist primarily to propagate the doctrines of a specific religion.

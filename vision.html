CrossCulture Community Foundation

Our Vision & Story

A movement of people living as apprentices of Jesus Christ — building CrossCulture Communities across Asia since 1982.

Foundational Vision

A Movement, Not an Organization

"CrossCulture Community is a movement of people living as apprentices of Jesus Christ, formed by the Word of God and led by the Spirit of God."

We are committed to lifelong transformation, giving our allegiance to Jesus Christ, and walking in obedience as His disciples. Through relationships, individuals and families form CrossCulture Communities — expressions of love and unity in the world, but not of the world.

Our hope is anchored in the promise of Jesus Christ. We live as faithful stewards, actively engaged in His mission, while eagerly awaiting His return and the fulfillment of His Kingdom.

Bill & Niki

A Life Called to Korea

1973

The Calling

At 13 years old, Bill Ray Majors surrendered his life to missions — a commitment that would shape every decade to follow.

1982

Arrival in Korea

Bill arrived in Korea at age 21, beginning what would become a 40+ year journey of ministry, community building, and cross-cultural service in Seoul.

1998

CrossCulture Community Begins

Foreigners visiting, working, and studying in Korea began gathering as CrossCulture Community — a movement that continues to grow across Asia.

2019

Foundation Established

CrossCulture Community Foundation registered as a 501(c)3 non-profit in Texas — creating a formal structure for receiving tax-deductible donations.

2026

Korea UNA Formed

크로스컬처 커뮤니티 (CrossCulture Community) established in Seoul, providing legal standing for on-the-ground ministry operations in Korea.

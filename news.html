Newsletter 2026

A New Chapter in 2026

This newsletter marks a significant moment in the CrossCulture Community story. After 40+ years of ministry in Seoul, we are entering a new chapter — one defined by organizational structure, a growing membership, and the vision of the Museum of the Bible Korea (MOTB-Korea) taking its first steps toward reality.

CrossCulture Community was formally established in Seoul in March 2026 as an Unincorporated Non-Profit Association. Three bilingual websites are now live. A membership system is in place. And the journey toward NPNGO registration — requiring 100 verified members — has begun.
